Intravitreal Aflibercept 8 mg - Overview

Summary

  • At this current time, 2 mg is the only approved dosage for Eylea (IVT-AFL) outside of the United States. Bayer does not recommend the use of its products in any manner inconsistent with approved product labeling.
  • Bayer and Regeneron are jointly investigating the safety and efficacy of IVT-AFL 8 mg in patients with nAMD and DME.
  • PULSAR is a randomized, double-masked, Phase 3 study to evaluate the efficacy and safety profile of IVT-AFL 8 mg vs 2 mg for the treatment of nAMD.1,2
  • PHOTON is a randomized, double-masked, Phase 2/3 study to evaluate the efficacy and safety profile of IVT-AFL 8 mg vs 2 mg for the treatment of DME.3,4
  • CANDELA is a randomized, multicenter, single-masked, Phase 2 study that evaluated the efficacy and safety profile of IVT-AFL 8 mg vs 2 mg in treatment-na√Øve patients with nAMD.5
