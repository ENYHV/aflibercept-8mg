Summary

  • At this current time, any dosage other than IVT-AFL 2 mg is not an approved dosage for Eylea. Bayer does not recommend the use of its products in any manner inconsistent with approved product labeling.

  • PHOTON is an ongoing, randomized, double-masked, Phase 2/3, 96-week study to evaluate the efficacy and safety profile of IVT-AFL 8 mg vs 2 mg for the treatment of DME.2
